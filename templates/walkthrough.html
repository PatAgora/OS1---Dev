Section 1: Staff Journey

End-to-end recruitment workflow from opportunity through to placement and ongoing management

Phase 1 — Opportunity & Engagement
1 Create Opportunity
Open the Opportunities page and create a new pipeline opportunity with client details, estimated value, and owner.
Lead Qualified Proposal Procurement Won
Route: /opportunities
Fields: Name, client, stage, owner, est. start, est. value, client contact details (name, role, phone, email).
Probability auto-set: Lead 0% → Qualified 10% → Proposal 30% → Procurement 50% → Won 100%.
Notes: Timestamped opportunity notes are tracked as an immutable audit trail.
2 Progress Stages
Move the opportunity through pipeline stages. Each stage change is logged with timestamp and user.
Route: /opportunity/<id>/edit
Stages: Lead → Qualified Lead → Proposal → Procurement → Closed Won / Closed Lost.
Audit: Every stage change creates an OpportunityNote record with the old and new stage.
3 Auto-Create Engagement
When an opportunity reaches Closed Won, the system automatically creates an Engagement with a sequential OS### reference.
Trigger: create_engagement_for_opportunity() fires on stage change to "Closed Won".
Auto-populated: Engagement ref (OS001, OS002...), name from opportunity, client, dates.
Link: opportunity_id on the Engagement creates a 1:1 relationship back to the opportunity.
4 Set Vetting Profile
Configure the engagement’s vetting requirements: which checks are needed, reference period, and gap threshold.
Route: /engagement/<id>/edit
Vetting checks: Right to Work, Identity Verification, Address History, DBS, Employment History, References, Qualifications, Professional Registration, Credit Check, Directorship, Sanctions/PEP, Social Media Review.
Profiles: Pre-saved vetting profiles can be loaded to auto-tick the right checks.
Config: Reference period (1–10 years, default 3), gap threshold days (default 90), IR35 status, required documents.
5 Create Job
Create a job under the engagement. Set role type, location, rate, IR35 status, and DBS/vetting specifics.
Route: /engagement/<eng_id>/job/create
Key fields: Title, description, role type, location, salary range, sector, IR35 (Inside/Outside), DBS level (Basic/Standard/Enhanced).
DBS extras: Employment sector, check purpose, position, employer name, job role, working with children/vulnerable adults, volunteer status, working from home.
Vetting overrides: Per-job JSON array of additional check types beyond the engagement defaults.
Public token: Auto-generated UUID for the public job advert URL.
Phase 2 — Applications & Pipeline
6 Job Goes Live
Job status set to “Open” — appears on the public portal job board. Associates can browse and apply.
Public URL: /public/jobs/<token>
Portal: Job board at /public/jobs lists all open jobs with search and filters.
IR35 displayed: Inside/Outside IR35 shown on the public advert so candidates know up front.
7 Associate Applies
Associate submits an application via the public portal with CV and cover note. Creates Application + Candidate records.
Route: /public/apply/<token>
Creates: Candidate record (if new email), Application record linked to job, uploaded CV stored as Document.
Status: Application starts at New → auto-moved to Pipeline.
8 AI Score & Summary
Gemini AI auto-generates a match score (0–100), summary, and explanation comparing the CV to the job description.
Route: /action/score/<id> and /action/summarise/<id>
AI fields: ai_score (integer), ai_summary (text), ai_explanation (detailed reasoning).
Auto-shortlist: If score exceeds threshold, candidate is auto-added to the shortlist for that job.
9 Review & Pipeline
Staff review applications in the pipeline view. Move candidates through stages:
Pipeline Shortlist I&A Client Offered Accepted
Views: /applications (list), /workflow (kanban), /application/<id> (detail).
Stage transitions: Controlled by ALLOWED_TRANSITIONS. Skip/unskip available for exceptional moves.
I&A stage: Schedule interview (date/time), send interview email, record interview notes and completion.
10 Interview & Assessment
At the I&A stage: schedule interview, send interview invitation email, record notes and outcome.
Actions: Schedule interview date, send email to candidate, mark interview completed, add interview notes.
Email: Interview invitation sent via SMTP with calendar details (TIMEZONE default: Europe/London).
Next: Move to Client Review for hiring manager feedback.
11 Make Offer
Send offer with capture sheet: start date, role title, day rate, location, engagement type, and expected duration.
Route: Offer modal on /application/<id>
Offer fields: Start date, role title, day rate (or salary), rate type, location, engagement type, expected duration, notes.
Email: Offer notification sent to associate’s email with portal link to respond.
Tracking: offer_made_at, offer_made_by_id, offer_response, offer_responded_at.
Phase 3 — Vetting & Contracts
12 Start Referencing
Trigger referencing: creates reference requests for each employment history entry. Sends emails to referees.
Route: /action/start-referencing/<cand_id>
Process: For each employment history entry, creates a ReferenceRequest with company name, referee email, and referee name.
Auto-lookup: If no referee email on the entry, checks the Reference Contact Details list for a known contact at that company.
Flagged houses: Cross-references against the Reference Houses Intel list — flagged companies get an orange warning and require additional verification.
Hold option: References can be created “on hold” (permission_status = no) if the candidate has asked to delay contact.
13 Start Full Vetting
Trigger all vetting checks. Sends commencement email to the associate with attachments. Triggers Verifile integration.
Route: /candidate/<cand_id>/start-vetting-with-email
Email: Vetting commencement email sent to associate — subject and body loaded from EmailTemplate (customisable). Supports file attachments.
Checks triggered: Up to 12 check types based on engagement vetting profile: Right to Work, ID Verification, Address History, DBS, Employment History, References, Qualifications, Professional Registration, Credit Check, Directorship, Sanctions/PEP, Social Media Review.
Verifile: DBS and employment screening orders created via Verifile API if configured.
14 Verifile Screening
External screening via Verifile: DBS checks, employment verification, qualification checks. Results update automatically via webhooks.
Integration: Verifile REST API for DBS (Basic/Standard/Enhanced), employment screening, qualification verification.
DBS data: Per-job configuration of employment sector, check purpose, position, employer name, job role, children/vulnerable adults flags.
Webhooks: /webhook/verifile receives status updates and auto-updates VettingCheck records.
15 Issue Contract
Capture assignment schedule details, download the filled Paystream template, and issue the contract for signature.
Capture sheet: Worker name, hirer, role title, start/end dates, fee, nature of work, hours, location, expenses, health & safety, vulnerable persons, notice periods, invoice frequency.
Template: Auto-populates the Paystream Assignment Schedule template from candidate/job/engagement data. Staff can override any field.
Route: /action/contract/capture/<app_id> → review → download filled template.
16 E-Signature
Send contract for electronic signature via Signable (Dropbox Sign) or DocuSign. Track sent/signed/completed status.
Providers: Configurable via ESIGN_PROVIDER env var — dropbox_sign or docusign.
ESigRequest model: Tracks application_id, provider, request_id, status (sent → signed → completed / declined), signer email, sent/signed timestamps.
Webhook: /webhook/esign receives signature events and auto-updates status.
17 Placed
Contract signed. Application marked as “Placed”. Candidate appears in the Placements view.
Route: /placements
Status: Application status = “Placed”. Visible in placements list, engagement dashboard, and reporting.
Financials: Placement data feeds into engagement financials and revenue reporting.
Phase 4 — Ongoing Management
18 Approve Timesheets
Review and approve submitted timesheets. Manage timesheet periods, hours, and notes per engagement.
Route: /admin/approvals
Model: Timesheet — user_id, engagement_id, period start/end, hours, status (Submitted/Approved/Rejected), notes.
Config: Timesheet configuration managed at /admin/timesheet-config.
19 Leave Requests
View scheduled leavers, manage leave requests, and track contractor availability across engagements.
Visibility: Dashboard shows upcoming end dates, scheduled leavers, and leave periods.
Alerts: Engagement dashboard highlights contractors approaching end dates.
20 Invoicing
Generate and manage invoices per engagement. Track amounts, due dates, and payment status.
Route: /admin/invoices
Model: Invoice — engagement_id, amount, status, due_date, line items (JSON), PDF generation.
Actions: Create, edit, view, download PDF, configure invoice settings.
21 Reporting
MI reporting: fill rates, time-to-hire, conversion rates, revenue tracking. Filter by client and engagement.
Route: /reporting
Metrics: Fill rates, time-to-hire averages, pipeline conversion rates, revenue per engagement, placement counts.
Filters: Client, engagement, date range. Export to CSV available on resource pool.

Section 2: Associate Journey

Candidate self-service from registration through onboarding, vetting, and ongoing contract management

A Register & Set Up Account
Register on the public portal with name and email. Set a secure password. Set up two-factor authentication (TOTP).
  • Register at /public/auth/signup
  • Set password (strength requirements enforced)
  • Configure 2FA via authenticator app (TOTP)
  • Receive and store backup codes for account recovery
Auth: Flask-Login with separate WorkerUser model for portal users. Session-based auth with CSRF protection.
2FA: pyotp TOTP with QR code setup. Backup codes generated (10 hex codes). Valid window of 1 period.
Security: Password history tracked (prevents reuse), account lockout after failed attempts, rate limiting on auth endpoints.
B Complete Profile
Fill in personal details, employment history (with company autocomplete and referee email lookup), qualifications, and upload documents.
  • Personal details: name, email, phone, address, date of birth
  • Employment history with company name autocomplete
  • Referee email lookup from Reference Contacts database
  • Qualifications and professional registrations
  • Document uploads: CV, proof of ID, proof of address, certificates
Company autocomplete: Searches existing Reference Contacts for company names, auto-populates referee email if a known contact exists.
Documents: Uploaded to /uploads/cvs/. Tracked in Document model with doc_type, filename, file_path.
Gap detection: Employment history checked for gaps exceeding the engagement threshold (default 90 days). Flagged for staff review.
C Company & Conduct Details
Choose engagement type: umbrella company or Ltd company. Complete conduct regulations declaration.
  • Select umbrella company from approved list, or declare Ltd Co
  • Provide company registration details if Ltd
  • Complete FCA conduct regulations declaration
Umbrella companies: Managed list at /admin/umbrella-companies. Associates select from the approved list.
Conduct regulations: Financial services specific — declaration required before placement in regulated roles.
D Browse & Apply for Jobs
Browse the public job board, view job details including IR35 status, and submit applications with a cover note.
  • Browse vacancies at /public/jobs
  • View full job description with IR35 status
  • Apply with cover note and CV upload
Public portal: Separate template set (templates_public/) with its own base layout.
Job board: Lists all jobs with status “Open”. Search by title, location, sector.
Application: Creates Application record linked to the Candidate and Job. Status starts at “New”.
E Receive & Respond to Offer
Receive offer notification via email. View offer details in the portal. Accept or decline with optional reason.
  • Email notification with link to portal
  • View offer: role, rate, start date, location, engagement type
  • Accept or decline (decline reason captured)
Offer fields visible: Role title, day rate/salary, start date, location, engagement type, expected duration, notes from staff.
Response tracked: offer_response (accepted/declined), offer_responded_at, offer_responded_ip, offer_decline_reason.
Audit: Response logged with IP address and timestamp for compliance.
F Complete Vetting
Receive vetting commencement email. Sign consent form, declarations, and complete Verifile checks via the portal.
  • Sign consent form (electronic)
  • Sign personal declaration
  • Complete secondary job declaration
  • Complete employment reference declaration
  • Complete Verifile identity and screening checks
Commencement email: Customisable template with associate name merge tags. Supports file attachments.
Forms: Consent form, personal declaration, secondary job declaration, employment reference declaration — all tracked as signed/unsigned.
Verifile: Candidate entry created via API. Associate completes identity verification and screening steps directly with Verifile.
G On Contract — Timesheets & Invoices
Once placed, submit weekly/monthly timesheets via the portal. Generate and download invoices.
  • Submit timesheets at /my/timesheets
  • Enter hours, period, and notes per engagement
  • View approval status (Submitted → Approved / Rejected)
  • Generate invoices based on approved timesheets
Timesheet model: user_id, engagement_id, period_start, period_end, hours, notes, status.
Approval flow: Associate submits → Staff reviews at /admin/approvals → Approve or reject with notes.
Invoicing: Invoice records linked to engagement with line items, amounts, due dates. PDF generation available.

Section 3: How They Connect

Key touchpoints where staff and associate workflows intersect

Application Submitted

ASSOCIATE applies via the public portal.
SYSTEM creates Application + Candidate records, runs AI scoring.
STAFF sees the application appear in the Pipeline / Workflow view with AI score and summary.

Offer Made & Accepted

STAFF creates offer with capture sheet details and sends notification email.
ASSOCIATE receives email, views offer in portal, accepts or declines.
SYSTEM records response with timestamp, IP, and moves application to “Accepted” stage.

Vetting Commenced

STAFF triggers vetting and sends commencement email with attachments.
ASSOCIATE receives email, signs consent/declaration forms, completes Verifile checks.
SYSTEM creates VettingCheck records, monitors progress, updates statuses via webhooks.

Referencing Started

STAFF triggers referencing from the candidate profile.
SYSTEM creates ReferenceRequest records for each employment entry. Auto-looks up referee emails from Reference Contacts. Flags known reference houses.
ASSOCIATE’s referees receive email requests directly.

Timesheet Cycle

ASSOCIATE submits timesheets via the portal with hours and notes.
STAFF reviews at Approvals page — approves or rejects with feedback.
SYSTEM updates status and feeds approved hours into invoicing.

Contract Signing

STAFF captures assignment schedule details and issues contract via e-signature provider.
ASSOCIATE receives signing request, reviews and signs electronically.
SYSTEM receives webhook on signature completion, updates ESigRequest status, moves application to “Placed”.

Scheduled Leavers

STAFF creates leave/end-date records for contractors approaching contract end.
SYSTEM shows upcoming leavers on the dashboard and engagement view.
ASSOCIATE’s contract end date tracked and visible in their portal.

Vetting Completion Gate

SYSTEM enforces that all 12 vetting checks must be Complete/N/A/QC Complete before allowing stage skip to “Placed”.
STAFF monitors vetting dashboard — each check shows status with colour coding.
ASSOCIATE’s vetting progress visible to staff in real time.
