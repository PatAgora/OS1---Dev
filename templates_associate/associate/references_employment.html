    Employment History Requirements

    Please read the following carefully before completing this page.

    Coverage Requirement
    • You must provide employment reference details covering the full 3 years prior to today's date.
    • All employment periods within this window must be listed, including part-time, contract, temporary, and agency work.
    • The entire 3-year period must be accounted for with no unexplained gaps.
    Employment Gaps
    • Any gap in employment exceeding 90 days must be declared and explained.
    • Supporting evidence is mandatory for each qualifying gap (e.g. P45, medical certificate, bank statement, official correspondence).
    • Evidence should be emailed to: compliance@optimussolutions.co.uk
    HMRC Employment Record
    • To help you complete this form accurately, you can obtain your official employment history from HMRC via the link below.
    • Your Personal Tax Account will show employer records including dates and PAYE references.
    • Access your HMRC employment record: www.gov.uk/get-proof-employment-history
    • Once downloaded, please also email your HMRC employment record to: compliance@optimussolutions.co.uk
    Permission to Contact
    • For each role, indicate whether Optimus has your permission to contact the referee immediately.
    • If No, you must provide a reason and either an earliest contact date or indicate TBC.
    Employment Reference Declaration

    Before any referencing can take place, you must sign the Employment Reference Declaration. This authorises Optimus to contact your previous employers on your behalf.
