    What documents are acceptable?
    Proof of Identity:
    • In-date Signed Passport
    • In-date UK Full Photo Card Driver's Licence
    Proof of Address:
    • Recent utility bill (last 3 months)
    • Bank or building society statement (last 3 months)
    • Council tax letter/bill (current year)
    • HMRC correspondence (last 12 months)

    This is not an exhaustive list. Please contact your Optimus representative to discuss alternative options.

    How long does the vetting process take?

    This will vary from person to person as we gather information from external sources. However, at the start of the vetting process, your Optimus representative will confirm the deadline for your assigned intake.

    What happens if a check is delayed or incomplete?

    Depending on what is missing, your Optimus representative may ask you to provide alternative evidence in the interim. We will use this evidence to refer your file to the client for approval. This helps us to onboard you for your intended start date.
